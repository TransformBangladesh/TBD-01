 Our Mission

Our core mission is to position Bangladesh as a leader in the global AI race. This is not just about technology; it's a crucial step towards the overall development and the realization of a truly Digital Bangladesh. We believe AI can empower our nation and its people by augmenting capabilities and driving innovation.

 The Challenge & Why We Need You

Making Bangladesh a significant player in the AI landscape is essential for our future progress. Falling behind is not an option. This ambitious project, initiated by an anonymous individual with a shared dream, requires the collective effort of passionate minds across Bangladesh.

We need your help to achieve this goal. This is a call for collaborators, innovators, students, professionals, and dreamers who want to work with AI and develop groundbreaking solutions relevant to our country's context.

 Who We Are Looking For

We are building a community—a team driven by shared interest and a vision for a technologically advanced Bangladesh. We seek individuals passionate about AI who want to learn, contribute, and build together. Please note, this is a collaborative, volunteer-driven initiative at this stage:

  • 
    Commitment Over Compensation: Currently, we cannot offer payment. Our initial focus is on building a strong foundation and proving potential. Contributions are based on shared interest and the desire to create a future that benefits all. We aspire to secure funding for compensation as the project progresses.
  • 
    Basic Tech Requirements: A computer capable of reasonably fast web browsing and a stable internet connection are necessary for collaboration and research.
  • 
    Highly Valued (But Not Required): Individuals with access to powerful computers, especially those equipped with capable Graphics Cards (GPUs), are strongly encouraged to join. AI development, particularly model training, demands significant computational resources. Your contribution here would be invaluable.
  • 
    Self-Driven Commitment: Your involvement should stem from genuine interest and a passion for AI and Bangladesh's development. Consistent, self-motivated contribution is key.

*Transparency Note: Building something ambitious from scratch involves risks, including the possibility of failure. Our primary goal is to foster a dedicated community, make tangible progress in AI applications relevant to Bangladesh, and attract the attention of potential partners and investors.*

 Call for Computational Power & Investment

Developing state-of-the-art AI models requires immense computational power (GPUs, TPUs, cloud resources), which we currently lack at scale. We are actively seeking multiple visionary investors and partners who believe in our mission. Investment is needed not just for hardware/cloud compute, but potentially for data acquisition, tooling, and operational support. Our goal is to leverage these resources to create AI that solves real-world problems for Bangladeshis.

 Our Vision for Bangladesh

  • Accelerating Digital Bangladesh: Contributing to the national goal through AI-driven solutions across sectors.
  • Empowering Humans: Designing AI systems that augment human intelligence and capabilities, not simply replace jobs.
  • Boosting Efficiency: Enabling individuals and organizations to achieve more with less effort through smart tools.
  • AI as a Tool for All: Promoting AI literacy and emphasizing its role as a powerful instrument for progress, education, and problem-solving.

*To those uncertain about AI's impact: We encourage you to research its potential benefits and challenges. Understanding this transformative technology is becoming increasingly important for everyone.*

 A Message from the Initiator

"I've always dreamed of creating impactful AI. As I researched, I realized the immense challenges—resource constraints, computational needs, and sometimes a lack of broader understanding, especially coming from a middle-class background here in Bangladesh. My family supported me as much as they could, but this vision requires more. Since I lack the personal funds to bootstrap this alone, I'm reaching out to build a community—people like you—who share this passion and believe in Bangladesh's potential. Let's build this future together, step by step."

 Important Disclaimer

This project is initiated with genuine and serious intent to contribute positively to Bangladesh's technological advancement. It is not designed to mislead anyone. As Bangladeshis, we believe in a shared desire for the betterment of our country, and this initiative is a reflection of that commitment. We aim for transparency throughout the process.
